About Us

Custom Solutions for Industrial Excellence

At RH Engineering Solutions Pvt Ltd, we specialize in designing, installing, and maintaining custom electrical systems and automated solutions for industrial machinery. With deep experience in fabrication equipment like CNCs, plasma cutters, and drilling machines, we ensure precision and uptime for your operations.

What sets us apart:

Full-cycle support from design to calibration

Expertise in analog/digital circuits and PCB assembly

Seamless integration of PLCs, control panels, and automation systems

Hands-on troubleshooting and preventive maintenance
